Delete Account - ADC Mart

Users of the ADC Mart app can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Email us at adctechsupport@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Delete my ADC Mart account
  3. Include your registered mobile number in the email.

Data deleted

We delete your account profile, registered mobile number, saved addresses, and app account data.

Data retained

Order records, invoices, payment records, fraud-prevention records, and legal/compliance records may be retained where required by law or business obligations.

Retention period

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Data required for legal, tax, security, or dispute purposes may be retained for the required retention period.
