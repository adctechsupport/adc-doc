Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Adc Mart collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide shopping, ordering, delivery, and customer support services.

  • Account information such as your name, mobile number, and login details.
  • Delivery information such as your address, house or flat details, landmark, city, area, and pincode.
  • Location information when you choose to use location services to detect or confirm your delivery address.
  • Order information such as products added to cart, placed orders, delivery status, and order history.
  • Device and technical information such as app version, device type, diagnostics, and basic usage data needed to improve reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Adc Mart account.
  • To confirm whether delivery is available at your address.
  • To process, pack, dispatch, and deliver your orders.
  • To use your mobile number for order updates, delivery coordination, customer support, and account verification.
  • To show relevant products, categories, offers, and app content.
  • To prevent fraud, misuse, and unauthorized activity.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

3. Location Information

Adc Mart may request access to your location to help fill or confirm your delivery address and check delivery availability in your area. We do not use location access for background tracking. You can disable location permission at any time from your device settings, but some delivery address features may not work correctly without it.

4. Mobile Number and Delivery Communication

We use your mobile number to identify your account, verify login, contact you about your orders, coordinate deliveries, and provide customer support. Delivery staff or service partners may use your mobile number only when required to complete or support your delivery.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary with:

  • Delivery partners or staff for delivering your orders.
  • Technology and hosting providers that help us operate the app.
  • Payment or transaction service providers, if online payment features are used.
  • Government, law enforcement, or legal authorities when required by applicable law.

6. Payments

If Adc Mart enables online payments, payment details may be processed by trusted payment service providers. We do not store your full card, bank, or payment credential details on our app servers.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration. However, no digital service can guarantee complete security.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as needed to provide our services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and maintain business records such as order history and delivery records.

9. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Update or correct your address and account information.
  • Disable location permission from your device settings.
  • Contact us to request account or data-related support.
  • Uninstall the app if you no longer want to use Adc Mart.

10. Children's Privacy

Adc Mart is intended for users who can place orders and provide valid delivery information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at adctechsupport@gmail.com.
